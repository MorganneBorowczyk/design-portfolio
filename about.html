Get to know me

Hi ! My name is Morganne Borowczyk. I am a design student at ECV Lille (France).

Before going back to school, I was a webdesigner at Wexperience, a web agency in Roubaix focused on UX design. My work there consisted of many things, including wireframing, front-end development, AB testing, analyzing focus groups and user testing.

This year I've decided to dedicate myself to learning again and to focus on the creative part of the work. Drawing has always been a big part of my life and I am very happy to be able to focus on more illustrated projects.

I've been doing a lot of work for bands of many styles! Rock, Folk, House music, it's always a pleasure to work with artists.

If you wish to make a commission please feel free to contact me hello@morganneborowczyk.design
